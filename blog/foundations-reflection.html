Foundations Reflections

29 September 2023

Values and programming

Three new things I learnt about myself in this and my ego during foundations are,

  • 1. I struggle with admitting I can’t do something and asking for help. It seemed like hardly anyone was asking for help in the discord channels and that put me off.
  • 2. I like a challenge, I enjoy struggling and having a moment of panic before pulling myself together and sorting my shit.
  • 3. I need to read instructions better. I could save myself a lot of stress by reading the whole set of instructions before starting the project.

Values, empathy and self-awareness are necessary in any job not just programming. They give us an opportunity to understand the person behind the job and realise at the end of the day no one is going to die if we don’t get a job finished.
