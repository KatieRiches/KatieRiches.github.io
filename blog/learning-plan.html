My learning plan

Long term pathway

My long term goal is to own my own business. I want to be time rich to be able to work remotely and choose when I work. To achieve this I want to work in a dev team to upskill for a few years and understand the industry. I have a specific interest in the agricultural industry and how technology can help this industry with climate issues.

Strengths and limitations

My strengths include that I have great time management skills. I like to avoid stress where possible so I have developed good organisation systems. I am also good at getting people back on track. When in group work I like to keep the situation project focussed so we can achieve the outcome.

A limitation I have is that if the work is not challenging or stimulating I struggle to maintain focus. I like to be working hard and achieving tangible outcomes so when a task appears more wishy washy I struggle to be as actively interested.

Skills to develop

Often I get so task orientated that people become a secondary skill. I am hoping through this course I will be able to develop a better human focused orientation.

Communication in the group

  • 1. Keep the Chat Flowing: Emojis, gifs, and memes are in the communication arsenal. 📱🤳 Listen up and reply ASAP; this ensures no one feels ignored got time for radio silence.
  • 2. Stay Chill and Organized: Use apps and tech to manage life. 📅📲 Make a to-do list, set priorities, and avoid last-minute stress.
  • 3. Spread Love, Not Drama: Handle issues with respect and openness. 💬❤️ If things get wild, bring in a mediator to keep the peace.
  • 4. Celebrate Diversity and Stay Real: Respect differences and make everyone feel like they belong. 🌍✌️ Dress to impress but keep it real, and stick to the code – low-key style.

How to deal with stress

Exercise is a fundamental aspect of my mental health. I train everyday and it is a good way to keep the stress out 🏋️‍♂️. When a problem is building up I like to take a break. Get away from the computer be it for a walk, some lunch or a breather.
